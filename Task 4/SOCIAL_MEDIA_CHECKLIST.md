# 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

A simple guide for college students to use social media safely, responsibly, and positively.

---

## ✅ Do’s
- 🔐 Use strong and unique passwords for every account  
- 🔒 Keep your privacy settings updated  
- 🧠 Think before posting or sharing content  
- 🌱 Share positive, helpful, and meaningful posts  
- 🤝 Be respectful and polite to others  

---

## ❌ Don’ts
- 🚫 Don’t share personal information (address, phone number, etc.)  
- ⚠️ Don’t post offensive or harmful content  
- 🔗 Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links  
- ❌ Don’t spread fake or unverified information  
- 📍 Don’t overshare your location or daily activities  

---

## 📊 Quick Checklist

| ✅ Do’s                         | ❌ Don’ts                          |
|--------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| Use strong passwords           | Share personal information       |
| Update privacy settings        | Post offensive content           |
| Think before posting           | Click suspicious links           |
| Share positive content         | Spread fake news                 |
| Respect others                 | Overshare location               |

---

## 📌 Note
Following these guidelines helps maintain a safe, respectful, and positive online presence.

---

## 📌 Conclusion
By following these simple do’s and don’ts, students can stay safe online, protect their privacy, and build a positive digital identity.
