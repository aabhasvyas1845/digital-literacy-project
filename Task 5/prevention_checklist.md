# 🛡️ Cyber Safety Guide: Protect Yourself Online

> ⚠️ **Note:** Cybercrime is increasing rapidly. Stay alert and follow these essential safety practices.

---

## 🔐 Basic Security Practices
- ✔ Create **strong, unique passwords** for each account  
- ✔ Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** wherever possible  
- ✔ Avoid clicking on **unknown or suspicious links**  
- ✔ Keep your **apps, software, and devices updated**

---

## 💳 UPI & Financial Safety
- ✔ Never approve **unknown payment or collect requests**  
- ✔ Do NOT share your **UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details**  
- ✔ Double-check **receiver details before sending money**  
- ✔ Avoid making transactions over **public Wi-Fi networks**

---

## 🧠 Smart Online Behavior
- ✔ Be cautious of **fake calls or messages pretending to be banks**  
- ✔ Avoid offers that seem **too good to be true**  
- ✔ Always verify **website URLs before logging in**  
- ✔ Log out after using **shared or public devices**

---

## 📱 Social Media Protection
- ✔ Avoid sharing **sensitive personal information publicly**  
- ✔ Regularly review and update **privacy settings**  
- ✔ Do not accept requests from **unknown individuals**

---
